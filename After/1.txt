Hindu
    Posing his 12th question to Prime Minister Narendra Modi as part of the series Gujarat Maange Jawaab [Gujarat seeks answers], targeting the BJP governments 22-year rule in the State, Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi wanted to know the state of small and medium-sized corporations in Gujarat. Mr. Gandhi said that while small and medium-level traders were facing troubles, the big corporates were in a happy state, adding that the GST and demonetisation had turned out to be a double whammy for the trading community. He said these two decisions had all but finished Gujarats business community, especially in Surat, Rajkpt, Alang and Anjar.
