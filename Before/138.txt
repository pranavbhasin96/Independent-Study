Iboyaima Laithangbam
  Senior Congress leader Phungzathang Tonsing is the latest sitting MLA to resign from the party and join the National Peoples Party (NPP) after spurning Congress ticket. NPP president C. Sangma welcomed him at a simple function on Saturday. With the entry of Tonsing, the NPP in Manipur will be strengthened, Mr. Sangma said. V. Valte, Nemcha Kipgen and several other MLAs who had been Ministers for two consecutive terms resigned from the Congress in the recent past. Most of them had been threatened by armed groups not to contest the upcoming elections on Congress ticket.  State Congress president T.N. Haokip said: "Politicians are known to change their loyalties. Numerically more people have joined us.
