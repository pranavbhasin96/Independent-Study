Special Correspondent
     Congress spokesperson Deepender Singh Hooda on Wednesday said party vice-president Rahul Gandhi will asking questions on BJPs 22-year Gujarat rule every day. He claimed the BJP has not made good on its promise of building 50 lakh houses for the poor as made in its election manifesto. We expect the Prime Minister to respond to these questions,said Mr. Hooda and alleged that the Modis governments economic policies have only helped the super rich and the middlemen. The Congress claimed real income for farners grew only by one percent and for non-agricultural workers, the figure is negative.
