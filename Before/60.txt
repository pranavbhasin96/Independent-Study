PTI
    Breaking its silence over Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhis charge that the Gujarat government extended undue favours of 33,000 crore to it for a car-manufacturing facility at Sanand, Tata Motors on Thursday said it got 584.8 crore as loan, not grant. Tata Motors built a plant in Sanand, with a vision to make it an automotive hub, thus contributing to the growth of India, the company said.
