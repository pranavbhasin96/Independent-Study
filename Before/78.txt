PTI
  About 9.61 lakh litres of liquor, 1.71 crore in cash and gold and jewellery worth over 8 crore have been seized by the Election Commission-appointed surveillance and expenditure monitoring teams in Gujarat till now. The EC teams have so far confiscated liquor valued at 22.19 crore from the State where prohibition is in force. The teams also seized suspected illicit cash totalling 1.71 crore, 37.63 kg of gold and other precious metals valued at 8.17 crore and 35 kg of narcotics worth 3.5 lakh. PTI
