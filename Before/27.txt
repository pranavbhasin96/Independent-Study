  The National Commission for Women (NCW) on Friday said it was examining a complaint against Patidar quota stir leader Hardik Patel over the purported sex videos of him that surfaced on social media.  Gaurav Gulati, a lawyer, sent a complaint to the Commisssion on November 22, asking the panel to investigate the matter and recommend the police to file a first information report against the guilty . We have received the complaint. We are currently seeking legal advice regarding the matter as the complaint has come from a third party, said an NCW spokesperson. PTI
