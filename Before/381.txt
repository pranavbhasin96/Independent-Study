PTI
  VADODARA: Senior Congress leader Digvijaya Singh, who is on 3,300 km Narmada Parikrama (circumambulation of the river Narmada), has entered Gujarat with his companions. Singh started his journey, on foot, from the Brahman Ghat at Narsinghpur in Madhya Pradesh on September 30. Talking to reporters after arriving at the Shoolpaneshwar temple near Sardar Sarovar dam at Kevadiya on Thursday, Singh said he wouldnt discuss politics. He would be walking through 20 assembly constituencies in pollbound Gujarat, including tribal-dominated ones like Nandod, Dediapada, Jhagadia and Netrang where polling would be held on December 9.
