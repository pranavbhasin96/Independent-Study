 Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi on Sunday cited a media report on a drop in the production of Nano cars at a Gujarat plant and said Prime Minister Narendra Modis pet Make in India project just died, turning Gujarati tax payers 33,000 crore into ash. Mr. Gandhi also asked who should be held accountable for the money becoming ash. The Congress vice-president has reportedly been saying that the Gujarat government had proffered benefits worth 33,000 crore to the car project in Sanand. PMs pet Make in India project just died. 33,000 crores of Gujarati taxpayer money turned to ash. Who is accountable? Mr. Gandhi said on Twitter.
