PTI
 AHMEDABAD: Prime Minister Narendra Modi would once again visit poll-bound Gujarat on December 3 and 4 and address seven rallies in Saurashtra and south Gujarat where polling will be held on December 9. On December 3, Modi would address campaign rallies in Bharuch in south Gujarat and Surendranagar and Rajkot in Saurashtra, state BJP in-charge Bhupender Yadav said here. Modi would also visit Ahmedabad in the evening to attend a function at Shree Swaminarayan Gurukul Vishwavidya Pratisthanam (SGVP). As per the SGVP website, Modi will inaugurate a newly built hospital on the SGVP campus. On the next day, Modi would address poll rallies at four places, starting from Dharampur in Valsad, followed by Bhavnagar, Junagadh and Jamnagar in Saurashtra, Yadav said. The prime minister addressed eight rallies on November 27 and 29 in Saurashtra and south Gujarat. The polling for 182-member Gujarat Assembly will take place on December 9 and 14 and the votes will be counted on December 18. Eighty-nine seats in Saurashtra and South Gujarat region are going to the polls in the first phase.
