PTI
 Senior NCP leader Praful Patel on Monday attacked Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the comments he had made on the election of the Congress president. Narendra Modi commented on infighting in the Congress, which was unwarranted. He had termed the election [of Congress president] as a tussle between Shezad and Shahzada, Mr. Patel said. Addressing a rally in Gujarat on Sunday, Mr. Modi had said that if one doesnt have democracy in his home (party), how can the person practice it in the country
