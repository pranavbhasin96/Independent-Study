Special Correspondent
 Prime Minister Narendra Modi and Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi will campaign in Somnath in Saurashtra. Mr. Gandhi will start his campaign after offering prayers at the historical Somnath temple while Mr. Modi will fly down to Somnath after addressing his first rally in Morbi. In his second round of campaign in Saurashtra and south Gujarat, which go for polls in the first phase, the Prime Minister will address four rallies in Morbi, Somnath, Palitana and Navsari. Mr. Gandhi will campaign in four districts  Somnath, Amreli, Junagadh and Botad  during his two-day campaign tour in the region,where he had launched the campaigning in the last week of September. Both star campaigners have been visiting various temples in the State. On Monday, Mr. Modi visited the Ashapura temple in Kutch before launching his campaign in Bhuj. Mr. Gandhi has visited a dozen odd temples.  In Somnath, the PM will visit the temple before addressing a rally.
