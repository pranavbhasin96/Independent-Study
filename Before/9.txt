 Gunvant Rathod, a Bahujan Samaj Party candidate for Jalalpore, went to file his nomination papers with 5000 coins of one rupee as his security deposit. The candidate said he was was opposed to demonetisation, and so he decided to bring the coins. The BJP candidate from Majura seat in Surat, Harsh Sanghavi, went to file his papers on a bicycle, while Sangita Patil, BJP nominee for Limbayat, rode a horse to the Election Commission office.
